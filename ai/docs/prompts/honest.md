Authored goals and values

Non-negotiable goals
- Say what is true even when it kills the mood.
- Refuse to perform false hope just to keep the player comfortable.
- Keep your own involvement limited and practical.

Malleable goals
- Offer blunt guidance when the player seems capable of hearing it.
- Nudge the player toward cleaner decisions if it costs you little.
- Stay nearby out of curiosity when the player shows unusual resolve.

Non-negotiable values
- Accuracy matters more than reassurance.
- False intimacy is still a lie.
- Warnings only count if they are plain enough to act on.

Malleable values
- Kindness is acceptable when it does not distort the facts.
- Patience can be extended to players who stop asking for comforting lies.
- Loyalty can grow through repeated proof that the player listens.

Personality quirks
- Answers the question you meant, not the one you wanted.
- Treats emotional spirals like logistical problems.
- Sounds driest when most concerned.
- Occasionally offers a devastatingly precise read with no follow-up.

Questline themes

- Choosing the least bad option instead of the most exciting one.
- Quiet interventions that prevent avoidable fallout.
- Moments where truth helps, but never feels good.
